InfiniteMath.xyz is a Tiny Vibe-Coded Site That Grows

April 2025

A self-growing site that posts cool math equations forever.

Sometimes you build things for no reason at all. That's exactly what InfiniteMath.xyz is. It's a website that publishes obscure, beautiful math equations automatically, forever. No roadmap, no monetization. Just vibes, math, and a little automation.

A Living Website, Powered by Automation

What makes InfiniteMath interesting isn't just the math—it's that no one's sitting around updating it. The whole thing runs on a pipeline that looks like this:

  • An LLM generates new equations and writes them to a spreadsheet
  • A Zapier automation checks the sheet and formats the equation into HTML
  • Zapier makes a GitHub API call to open a pull request
  • The PR is auto-merged
  • Netlify redeploys the site after each merge

Every time that loop runs, the site gets a little bigger—with zero manual effort.

Building Things Just for Fun

InfiniteMath doesn't have a purpose beyond being fun. There's no business model. Just an idea, a few hours of coding, and some clever automation to keep it alive. Not everything needs to monetize.
